#

**2. If you don't know the password:**\
Sometimes, your Mac login password doesn't work because it got out of sync. You can reset the keychain entry: [[1](https://www.reddit.com/r/chrome/comments/1ema3ya/chrome_safe_storage_password/), [2](https://discussions.apple.com/thread/253741610), [3](https://www.justanswer.com/computer/rc8xx-tried-install-duck-duck-go-stymied-asked-tfor.html)]

1. Completely **Quit Google Chrome**.
2. Open your Mac's **Finder**, click **Applications**, and open the **Utilities** folder.
3. Open the **Keychain Access** app.
4. Type `Chrome Safe Storage` into the search bar in the top right corner.
5. **Right-click** (or hold Control and click) the "Chrome Safe Storage" item and select **Delete**.
