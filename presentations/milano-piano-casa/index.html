Why Previous Bonifica Projects Failed

The Pattern: 155-500% Cost Overruns

Multiple Milano bonifica projects have failed or been abandoned due to dramatic cost explosions:

  • Sant'Elia Olympics: €18M → €46M (+155%)
  • Bovisa MoLeCoLa: €10M → €50M (+400%)
  • Campus Goccia: Bonifica alone €50M

Why Estimates Fail

  • Inadequate initial site characterization
  • Worst contamination found during work, not before
  • No public access to technical reports
  • Complex Milano industrial legacy

Warning Signal (May 2024)

Campus Goccia tender: ZERO bidders

Contractors see Milano bonifica as too risky. Only 6% of Italian sites remediated in 30 years.
