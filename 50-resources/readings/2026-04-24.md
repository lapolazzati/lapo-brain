### **Money Stuff: Traders Do Something About the Weather** (Matt Levine)
*Saved: Apr 23, 2026*

Financial markets for bets and derivatives sometimes become bigger than the real things they're based on, creating strange feedback loops. Prediction markets like Polymarket and Kalshi attempt to forecast events but also influence how people act and how markets function.

**Two approaches to regulation:**
- *Polymarket*: Ignored US rules, focused internationally, eventually faced enforcement
- *Kalshi*: Worked with regulators, slower but more sustainable

**Meta-market effects:** When derivatives market exceeds underlying market size, the tail wags the dog - markets designed to predict reality start shaping it instead.
