## Response Patterns Observed

| Contact | Response Type | What Triggered It | Next Move |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|-----------|
| Dott. Masi | No reply yet (as of May 30) | Formal technical pitch | Follow-up in 5-7 days with one-pager attached |
| Rudi Ruggeri | "Send brochure first" | Casual tone, clear value prop | Sent brochure, now waiting for him to review |
| Michele Remonti | Strong positive, asked for materials + call | Deep technical detail (BioBlend, oxidation, phytoremediation) + asked for judgment | Sent materials, proposed meeting with US partners |

**Key insight:** The Remonti email performed best because it offered the contact a role as *expert judge* rather than just *potential customer*. People respond when you ask for their opinion, not just their business.
