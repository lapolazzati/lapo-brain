## Overview

K3 Ventures is a venture capital firm based in Singapore, focusing on early-stage technology companies. Founded to support visionary founders, K3 Ventures invests in startups that aim to tackle significant global challenges. The firm operates primarily in Asia, emphasizing collaboration with entrepreneurs to foster growth and innovation.

Currently, K3 Ventures manages a portfolio of seven notable companies, showcasing its commitment to various sectors, including agtech, consumer products, edtech, SaaS, and healthcare. The firm has established itself as a key player in the Southeast Asian market, leveraging its strategic location to connect with emerging technology hubs.

With a team of dedicated professionals, K3 Ventures aims to provide not only capital but also operational support and mentorship to its portfolio companies.
