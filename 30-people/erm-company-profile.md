# ERM — Company Deep Dive

**ERM = Environmental Resources Management**
- **Position:** World's largest pure-play sustainability consultancy
- **Size:** 8,000+ people in 40+ countries, 140+ offices
- **Founded:** 1971 (50+ years experience)
- **HQ:** UK (presumed — British firm)
- **Website:** erm.com

## Italian Presence

- **Office:** Milan (opened 1992)
- **People:** 160+ employees
- **Managing Director Italy:** Francesco Ducco
- **Partner Italy:** Chiara Sacco
- **Certifications:** ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001
- **Compliance:** "231 Organisational Model" (Legislative Decree 231/2001)
- **Coverage:** Energy, Financial Services, Chemical, Technology/Media/Telecom, Mining & Metals

## Core Capabilities (Relevant to CEG)

- Remediation Management
- Site investigation, remedial strategy, implementation
- PFAS and emerging contaminants
- Human health & ecological risk assessment
- Forensic & environmental chemistry
- Decommissioning, decontamination, demolition
- Environmental liability assessment
- Portfolio management (legacy liabilities)

- Develop remediation strategies for PFAS globally

## Why ERM Matters for CEG

1. **Scale:** ERM is the 800-lb gorilla. If they're interested in a PFAS project or terrain, they have the capital and reach to execute.
2. **Acquisitive:** Newfields acquisition shows they buy into remediation capabilities. If CEG has a compelling tech/methodology, ERM could be a buyer or partner.
3. **Belgium connection:** Sustainalize gave them Belgium presence. Belgian PFAS project (mentioned in Remonti outreach) is in their geographic footprint.
4. **Complementary, not competitive:** CEG's in-situ bioremediation is a specific technology. ERM is a consulting/project management layer. Natural partnership if the technology is proven.
