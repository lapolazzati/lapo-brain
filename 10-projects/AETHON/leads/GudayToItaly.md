# GUDAY × Italy — TikTok Shop Expansion Lead

**Source:** K3 (invested in GUDAY.br)\
**Research date:** 2026-06-09\
**Model:** Find an Italian co-founder for GUDAY Italia. TikTok Shop as sole distribution channel.

***

## GUDAY in 30 seconds

Brazilian creatine gummies brand co-founded by **Manuela Cit** under the NORTE group. She's not an ambassador — she co-owns it. That structure is why it works. The product: creatine monohydrate in gummy format, Gen Z-coded, female-friendly, 4 flavors.

Italian DTC already exists (`mkpbr.com/en-it/`, Creatine €101.95) with zero Italian marketing. The distribution bet: **TikTok Shop only**. No retail, no Amazon.

## The honest gap

The Italian "Manuela Cit" — Gen Z, female, TikTok-native, gym-focused, entrepreneurial, no supplement exclusive — does **not appear at scale in public data**. The Italian fitness TikTok space is older than the Brazilian one (most creators are 30–35), and the gym/creatine niche specifically is underserved by female Italian creators.

This is the opportunity and the problem. GUDAY can own this space because nobody else does. But it means the co-founder won't come from a "top 10 Italian fitness TikToker" list.

***

## How to find her

The right person is probably at 100–400K TikTok right now, building. She won't be in a ranking.

**Three channels to search:**

1. **Italian influencer agencies** — ICM, YOUparti, Oneshot, Buzzoole. They know who's growing before it's public.
2. **TikTok Italy search directly** — query: `#creatina`, `#palestra`, `#fitnessitalia`, `#supplementi`, `#routine` — look for female creators under 27 with 100K+ views per video and comment quality (not just likes).
3. **Ask NORTE/GUDAY** — they've done this once. They know the profile. Ask who they'd scout if they were doing Italy themselves.
