## The Challenge (Problem Framing)

**Stat:** 87% of CFOs say AI is critical. Only 21% report seeing measurable results.

**Three pain points:**

1. **Fragmented Data.** Messy and siloed data across dozens of systems: custom ERPs, a chart of accounts inherited through acquisitions, PDF policies, and context that exists in people's heads.

2. **Lack of Context.** Generic AI doesn't understand your business. Without domain knowledge built in, it produces responses that look right but aren't reliable enough to act on.

3. **Change Management.** Teams are busy doing their day jobs. Becoming AI-native requires a fundamental cultural shift and operational redesign that most organizations can't drive on their own.

## The Ramp Method (Solution Framework)

Three pillars:

1. **Solve the Data Problem.** Our engineers embed with your finance team to map your data to how your business actually runs, then build agents that read and write into systems already in place.

2. **Domain Expertise.** Ramp processes $200B+ annually for 70,000+ businesses, giving us a working view of how finance operates at scale: vendor patterns, month-end bottlenecks, and where workflows break.

3. **Cost Control.** Ramp is model-agnostic by design. We select the right model and provider for each task based on performance and cost per outcome.

## Active Solutions (Product/Use Cases)

Four specific AI agents/workflow automations:

### 1. Turn your GL into a live management view
- **Problem:** GAAP closes the books. It doesn't run the business.
- **Solution:** Real-time management layer so your CFO can answer operational questions without waiting for the monthly close.

### 2. Find the rule. Cite the source
- **Problem:** Real policy isn't a flowchart. It's exceptions buried in PDFs and someone's email from 2019.
- **Solution:** An adjudication agent reads the library, picks the binding rule, and cites the source.

### 3. Reconcile every contract line, every invoice
- **Problem:** Procurement contracts are 50-300 pages of nested pricing logic your AP team can't cover manually.
- **Solution:** A reconciliation engine reads every line, matches every invoice, and flags what doesn't add up.

### 4. Place every asset in the right facility
- **Problem:** Complex financing structures spread new assets across 10-20 facilities, each with its own constraints. Portfolio managers do it manually.
- **Solution:** An allocation engine scores every facility and picks the right one.

## Process / Engagement Model

**Four-step delivery:**

1. **Discovery** — We embed with your finance team to map how work moves across systems and decisions, and where the leverage lives.
2. **Deploy** — We build a working agent against a tightly scoped workflow and ship it to production. Your team sees ROI before we expand.
3. **Empower** — Your team takes ownership of what we built together. We document, train, and stay close as adoption grows.
4. **Adapt** — As the business evolves, we update the logic and ensure your system runs on the most cost-efficient models.
