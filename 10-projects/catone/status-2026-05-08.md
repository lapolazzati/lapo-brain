## Auto-Onboard System

### What It Does

**Flow:**
1. Lead fills Google Form (email, name, studio, P.IVA)
2. Apps Script webhook calls `POST /api/onboard`
3. System creates Organization + User + Magic Link
4. Email sent via Resend
5. Lapo gets Telegram notification
6. Lead clicks magic link → inside Catone in 60 seconds

**Benefit:**
- Gianni distributes form link via WhatsApp
- Zero manual onboarding from Lapo
- Scales to 100+ signups/month
- Lead gets access in 30 seconds (vs hours/days waiting for manual approval)
