# Bioremediation Resources

## Knowledge

- [Book: *Toms River: A Story of Science and Salvation* — Dan Fagin](https://www.amazon.com/Toms-River-Science-Salvation-Fagin/dp/055380653X)
  Pulitzer-winning single-site detective story covering chemistry, hydrogeology, epidemiology, and regulation. Closest thing to a CEG case study in book form. Use for: understanding how contamination, remediation, and regulation interact in a real Italian-style scenario.

- [Book: *Napoleon's Buttons: 17 Molecules That Changed History* — Le Couteur & Burreson](https://www.amazon.com/Napoleons-Buttons-Molecules-Changed-History/dp/1585423319)
  Each chapter = one molecule + its story. You learn organic chemistry by accident. Use for: building intuitive feel for molecular structure and bond chemistry without a textbook.

- [Book: *Periodic Tales* — Hugh Aldersey-Williams](https://www.amazon.com/Periodic-Tales-Curious-Lives-Elements/dp/0061824733)
  Element-by-element storytelling. Read chapters on chlorine, arsenic, lead, mercury — your core contaminants. Use for: understanding why specific elements behave the way they do in the environment.

- [Book: *Silent Spring* — Rachel Carson](https://www.amazon.com/Silent-Spring-Rachel-Carson/dp/0618249060)
  Best explanation of bioaccumulation, persistence, and metabolic pathways in plain English. Use for: understanding why some contaminants stick around and magnify through food chains.

- [Book: *In Situ Chemical Oxidation for Groundwater Remediation* — Siegrist, Crimi, Simpkin (SERDP/ESTCP)](https://www.amazon.com/Chemical-Oxidation-Groundwater-Remediation-Technology/dp/1441978299)
  The ISCO bible — exactly what CEG does with Advanced Oxidation. Read Chapter 2 (reaction chemistry) + Chapter 5 (persulfate). Use for: understanding the actual chemistry behind CEG's core technology.

- [Book: *Bioremediation: Applied Microbial Solutions* — Atlas & Philp](https://www.amazon.com/Bioremediation-Applied-Microbial-Solutions-Environmental/dp/1555812392)
  Clearest intro to biostimulation (CEG BioBlend's approach). Chapter on hydrocarbon degradation is gold. Use for: understanding how adding nutrients wakes up native bacteria.

- [Book: *Bonifica dei siti contaminati* — Beretta, Pellegrini (Hoepli)](https://www.hoepli.it/libro/bonifica-dei-siti-contaminati/9788820374638.html)
  Standard Italian reference on contaminated site remediation. Use for: Italian regulatory context, D.Lgs. 152/2006 procedures, ARPA workflow.

- [Guidelines: ISPRA "Criteri Metodologici per l'applicazione dell'analisi assoluta di rischio ai siti contaminati" — Rev. 2, 2008](https://www.isprambiente.gov.it/contentfiles/00001800/1819-1960-linee-guida.pdf)
  The Italian technical bible for contaminated site risk analysis. Use for: understanding the regulatory framework ARPA applies, conceptual site model construction, forward/backward risk analysis.

- [Database: MOSAICO — ISPRA National Contaminated Sites Database](https://www.isprambiente.gov.it/it/attivita/suolo-e-territorio/siti-contaminati)
  Government-tracked database of all Italian contaminated sites. Use for: understanding the market, finding precedent cases, filtering by contaminant type and regulatory stage.

- [Reference: EPA CLU-IN — Technology Focus pages](https://clu-in.org/)
  Free webinars and tech sheets on every remediation technology. Use for: quick 20-minute reads per contaminant class when you need a fast orientation.

- [YouTube: Practical Engineering — Grady Hillhouse](https://www.youtube.com/@PracticalEngineeringChannel)
  Visual, 10-15 minute episodes on groundwater, contamination plumes, landfills. Use for: building intuition about how contamination moves underground.

- [Film: *Erin Brockovich* (2000)](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0195685/)
  Chromium-6 contamination case study. Use for: understanding hexavalent chromium chemistry and health effects through story.

- [Film: *Dark Waters* (2019)](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt9071322/)
  DuPont/PFAS contamination case study. Use for: understanding PFOA chemistry and why fluorinated compounds are so persistent.

- [CEG Internal: PFAS Hybrid Remediation Infrastructure Analysis (Douglas Klopp, May 2026)](../../technologies/pfas-hybrid-remediation-summary.md)
  Validates hybrid technology approach and WaaS business model. Use for: understanding how biological + chemical + electrochemical work together; the economics of infrastructure deployment vs. one-time sales.

- [CEG Internal: CEG Italia Strategic Notes (May 2026)](../ceg-italia-strategic-notes.md)
  Market context: 38,556 bonifica procedures, 16,365 ongoing. Use for: understanding why the Italian market is CEG's opportunity.

- [CEG Internal: ISPRA Guidelines Summary (May 2026)](./ispra-guidelines-summary.md)
  Detailed breakdown of ISPRA risk analysis methodology, CSC/CSR distinction, and ARPA workflow. Use for: regulatory conversation preparation.
