# CEG Outreach — Corrected Target Levels

**Insight:** Cold outreach to CEOs/Board Presidents of €30-170M companies has <1% reply rate. The person who evaluates new bioremediation tech is rarely the CEO. Target the *technical buyer* — someone who understands the science AND can recommend it upward.

---

## Company Types & Right Target Level

| Company Type | Revenue | Right Target | Wrong Target |
|-------------|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Family-owned SME | €10-50M | CEO/Owner (they're operationally involved) | No one else has authority |
| Structured mid-size | €50-170M | Technical Director, Division Head, Env. Manager | Board President, CEO cold email |
| PE-backed / Large | €170M+ | Innovation Manager, R&D Head, Technical Evaluator | CEO, CFO |

## Target Level Rules

1. **Family business (€10-50M):** CEO/Owner is fine — they're operationally involved
2. **Structured company (€50-170M):** Target Technical Director, Division Head, Env. Manager — NOT Board/CEO
3. **Large/PE-backed (€170M+):** Target Innovation/R&D, Technical Evaluator, or Procurement — need warm intro for C-level
4. **For ALL cold outreach:** The ideal target has BOTH technical expertise AND internal influence. They can say "I found this, let's evaluate it" to their boss.
