# CEG Italy

Italian market entry for Continuum Environmental Group's bioremediation technology.

## One-liner

Bring CEG's bioremediation tech to Italian brownfield/contaminated-site market.

## Why this matters

Equity bet. Slower clock than Catone, but real. Italy has a deep contamination backlog and a regulated framework (D.Lgs. 152/2006) that creates predictable demand.

- Strategic mapping done: regulatory framework, competitive landscape, revenue scenarios, 18-month execution plan
- Fastest path to revenue: paid feasibility studies (€5–15K) and decommissioned fuel station projects
- Catone-CEG flywheel thesis: commercialisti see contaminated property transactions early → warm intros to brownfield owners
