## Event Overview

### Purpose
Brings together remediation scientists, industrial operators, and institutional bodies from Europe and worldwide. Focus on bioremediation and environmental restoration processes.

### Key Components
- Two-track program
- ALL4BIOREM workshop (European Bioremediation Projects cluster)
- Scientific results and technology implementation showcase
- Cross-initiative partnerships among research institutions and stakeholders
