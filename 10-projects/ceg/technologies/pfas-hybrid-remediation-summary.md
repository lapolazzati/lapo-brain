## System Architecture (5 Stages)

### 1. Subsurface Interception
- Permeable reactive barriers (PRBs) capture plumes
- Foam fractionation + hybrid electro concentration
- **Key insight:** Concentration improves downstream economics

### 2. Sealed Ex-Situ Treatment
- Physically isolated infrastructure
- Stainless-steel reactors, anaerobic digesters, BES modules
- Regulatory compatibility through containment

### 3. Biological Reduction
**Acidimicrobium sp. A6:** 40-60% removal over 100 days (autotrophic, iron-rich anaerobic)

**GENIA Synthetic Consortia (2025):** 93.1% PFOS removal in 7 days
- Pseudomonas pergaminensis (top performer)
- 2-4× improvement vs. monocultures
- Clear defluorination (22 mg/L fluoride liberated)

**Target:** >80% total organic fluorine reduction

### 4. Hybrid Bioelectrochemical Systems
- Electroactive biofilms on same electrodes as polishing
- Up to 96% removal efficiency
- 20-40% lower energy vs. standalone electrochemical

### 5. Final Mineralization
- Calcium fluoride precipitation
- Circular economy: fluoride recovery as industrial feedstock
- Reduces long-term liability

## Verification & Regulatory Strategy

### Analytical Verification
- LC-MS/MS PFAS profiling (EPA Method 1633)
- Total Organic Fluorine (TOF) analysis
- **Metagenomics + qPCR** (A6 rdhA gene, dehalogenases)
- **Stable isotope probing (SIP) + ¹⁹F-NMR** (mechanism-level proof)

### Regulatory Approach
**Not circumvention - accelerated deployment within existing frameworks**
- Physical containment
- Auditable destruction verification
- Independent third-party validation
- EPA/CERCLA/DoD compatibility
