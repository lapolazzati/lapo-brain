## Research Notes

### Categoria 9 Market Overview

**Certification Requirements:**
- 5 classification classes based on project cost
- Class A: Projects over €9M (requires 5+ years experience)
- Registration valid 5 years, renewable
- Financial guarantees required
- Technical competence demonstration

**Total Market:**
- Hundreds of Categoria 9 holders in Lombardy
- ~1,000 active contaminated sites in Lombardy
- 3,184 sites already remediated (potential repeat customers)
- 5 Sites of National Interest (SIN) in Lombardy: Sesto San Giovanni ex Falck, Pioltello Rodano, Broni ex Fibronit, Brescia Caffaro, Mantova Polo Chimico

**PNRR Funding:**
- €65M for 18 orphaned sites in Lombardy (€51M PNRR + €14M regional)
- 18 sites in Milan, Como, Lecco, Monza Brianza, Sondrio, Varese
- Target: 70% of orphan site surface remediated by Q1 2026
- June 30 deadline for PNRR structural milestones
- Examples: Sesto San Giovanni Viale Italia 936, Vimodrone Area Nova-Elfe

**Average Project Economics:**
- €11M bonifica budgets in recent Milan tenders
- €3.6M average PNRR-funded project
- Municipality covering costs = more viable projects = more bidders

**Common Pain Points Identified:**
1. **PNRR Compliance:** Tight timelines (29 consecutive days), specific milestones, detailed reporting
2. **Municipal Reporting:** Transparency requirements, progress tracking, document management
3. **Environmental Monitoring:** Soil/groundwater data, contamination characterization, post-remediation verification
4. **Regulatory Compliance:** D.Lgs. 152/2006, ARPA Lombardia coordination, Municipality approvals
5. **Multi-Site Coordination:** Companies managing multiple simultaneous projects
6. **ISO Certification Overhead:** ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, EMAS documentation
7. **Asbestos-Specific:** Category 10 compliance, mapping, worker safety documentation
8. **Technology Gap:** 60+ year old firms using legacy/manual processes
