## What they do

Preventive health platform. Users get blood tests covering 110+ biomarkers (metabolic, hormones, cardiovascular, inflammation, micronutrients). AI companion app cross-references results with medical history and lifestyle to generate personalized recommendations in nutrition, supplementation, and behavior change. Clinical oversight by 10+ physicians.

## Traction

- 10,000+ users across France, UK, Ireland, Portugal
- 1M+ biomarker tests run
- 75% of users with 6-month follow-up improved ≥3 biomarkers without medication
- 80%+ retest rate
