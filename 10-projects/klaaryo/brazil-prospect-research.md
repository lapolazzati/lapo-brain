## Key Findings: ATS Platform Dominance in Brazil

### Primary ATS Platforms Identified

1. **Gupy** - DOMINANT PLATFORM
   - Used by: Pague Menos, Lojas Renner, Riachuelo, C&A, Grupo Boticário
   - Market position: Leading enterprise ATS in Brazil
   - Features: AI-driven recruitment, large job portal
   - Company profile: Brazilian startup, became major job portal by 2026

2. **Pandapé** - STRONG PRESENCE
   - Used by: Bacio di Latte, Spot Promo, Óticas Carol (secondary)
   - Market position: HR software suite with AI and predictive analytics
   - Founded: 2018, São Paulo
   - Features: Recruitment + personnel management

3. **InfoJobs** - JOB BOARD + ATS
   - Used by: Bacio di Latte (with Pandapé), Poptrade
   - Market position: Job board aggregator with ATS features
   - Common for multi-channel recruitment

4. **Enlizt** - NICHE PLAYER
   - Used by: Óticas Carol (primary)
   - Market position: Smaller/specialized ATS
   - Less common among large retailers

5. **Other Platforms**
   - **Vagas.com**: Drogaria São Paulo, Drogarias Pacheco
   - **InHire**: Magazine Luiza
   - **LinkedIn**: Used by most as secondary/supplementary platform
   - **Glassdoor/Indeed**: Job aggregators, some direct posting

### Multi-ATS Usage Patterns

**Companies Using Multiple Platforms:**
- Bacio di Latte (Pandapé + InfoJobs)
- Poptrade (InfoJobs + LinkedIn)
- Spot Promo (Pandapé + Job Boards)
- Óticas Carol (Enlizt + Pandapé)
- Magazine Luiza (InHire + InfoJobs + LinkedIn)
- Chilli Beans (Multiple platforms)
- Vivara (LinkedIn + Multiple)
- Cacau Show (Multiple platforms)

**Why Multiple Platforms?**
1. Broader candidate reach across different talent pools
2. Different platforms for different role types (corporate vs. store operations)
3. Regional coverage variations
4. Franchise vs. corporate hiring needs
5. Supplementing primary ATS with job boards for visibility

## Industry-Specific Observations

### Pharmacy Sector
- **Dominant ATS**: Gupy (Pague Menos uses it exclusively)
- **Company Size**: Very large (20K-74K employees)
- **Hiring Volume**: High (300-1,000+ positions)
- **Trend**: Consolidation toward Gupy for enterprise pharmacy chains

### Fashion Retail Sector
- **Dominant ATS**: Gupy (Renner, Riachuelo, C&A all use it)
- **Company Size**: Large (26K-42K employees)
- **Hiring Volume**: Very high (800-2,600+ positions)
- **Trend**: Strong standardization on Gupy

### Specialty Retail (Ice Cream, Chocolate, Optical)
- **Dominant ATS**: More fragmented - Pandapé, Enlizt, Multiple platforms
- **Company Size**: Smaller to medium (1K-16K employees)
- **Hiring Volume**: Moderate (10-300 positions)
- **Trend**: Higher likelihood of using multiple platforms

### Promotional/Trade Marketing
- **Dominant ATS**: Pandapé, InfoJobs, Multiple
- **Company Size**: Medium (5K-9K promoters)
- **Hiring Volume**: High (100-280+ positions)
- **Trend**: Multi-platform approach due to high-volume, distributed workforce

## Klaaryo GTM Implications

### High-Priority Segments

1. **Large Pharmacy Chains** (Gupy dominant)
   - Pain point: Managing 300-1,000+ positions across all Brazilian states
   - Multi-ATS opportunity: Low (standardized on Gupy)
   - Value prop: Onboarding automation for high-volume hiring

2. **Fashion Retail Chains** (Gupy dominant)
   - Pain point: 800-2,600+ positions, multi-brand ecosystems
   - Multi-ATS opportunity: Moderate (Gupy + job boards)
   - Value prop: Multi-brand onboarding orchestration

3. **Specialty Retail** (Fragmented ATS)
   - Pain point: Franchise + corporate hiring complexity
   - Multi-ATS opportunity: HIGH (actively using 2-3 platforms)
   - Value prop: Central onboarding hub across ATS platforms

4. **Promotional Marketing** (Pandapé + Multiple)
   - Pain point: 5K-9K promoter workforce, high turnover
   - Multi-ATS opportunity: HIGH (multi-platform by necessity)
   - Value prop: Rapid onboarding for distributed workforce

### Strategic Recommendations

1. **ATS Integration Priority:**
   - Tier 1: Gupy (must-have - dominates large employers)
   - Tier 2: Pandapé (strong in specialty retail, growing)
   - Tier 3: InfoJobs, Vagas.com, InHire (supplementary)

2. **Target Profile:**
   - Companies with 1,000+ employees
   - 100+ open positions simultaneously
   - Multiple store locations across Brazil
   - Evidence of multi-ATS usage (highest conversion potential)

3. **Messaging Angles:**
   - "Unify onboarding across Gupy, Pandapé, and job boards"
   - "Onboard 1,000+ hires per year with consistency"
   - "Franchise + corporate onboarding in one platform"
   - "Reduce time-to-productivity for distributed workforce"
