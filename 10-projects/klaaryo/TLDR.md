# Klaaryo Expansion Research - TL;DR

*Prepared for Fede call - May 5, 2026*

---

## 🎯 Bottom Line

**Brazil is the #1 opportunity.** Poland #2. Spain and Portugal are viable but smaller.

---

## 🇧🇷 Brazil - ★★★★★ (MASSIVE OPPORTUNITY)

**Why it's huge:**
- 139M WhatsApp users (2nd globally) - 91% internet penetration
- 36% annual turnover crisis (record high)
- Retail/franchising desperate: McDonald's (800 stores), Cacau Show (1,600 stores)
- USD 454M recruitment software market by 2035

**Key competitors:**
- Gupy (market leader, 4,000 clients, but enterprise-focused)
- Sólides (40,000 SMB clients, recently added WhatsApp)
- Clear gap: No dominant WhatsApp-native solution for mid-market

**Quick win sectors:**
1. Retail/franchising (1.2M positions/year)
2. Manufacturing (high-volume hiring)
3. Services (53% of new jobs)

**Entry strategy:** Start São Paulo, target 10-15 retail/franchise chains Year 1

---

## 🇵🇱 Poland - ★★★★☆ (STRONG OPPORTUNITY)

**Why it's compelling:**
- 800,000 vacant positions despite 5% unemployment
- 34% already using recruitment automation (ready market)
- 92% WhatsApp penetration
- EU AI Act creates compliance advantage

**Key competitors:**
- InteliWISE (local player, enterprise focus)
- Traffit (Polish ATS, 5,000 users, affordable)
- Paradox (global enterprise)
- Gap: WhatsApp-native virtually absent

**Quick win sectors:**
1. Logistics (most acute shortage, multilingual needs)
2. Retail (Biedronka 81K employees, Dino 47K)
3. Manufacturing (aging workforce)

**Entry strategy:** Q2-Q3 2026 optimal timing, pilot with 2-3 logistics/retail companies

---

## 🇪🇸 Spain - ★★★☆☆ (SOLID OPPORTUNITY)

**Why it works:**
- 90% WhatsApp penetration
- Tourism: 2.7M jobs, 97M visitors (seasonal hiring nightmare)
- Youth unemployment 23.8% (talent available, hiring inefficient)

**Key competitors:**
- Factorial HR (Barcelona unicorn, 15,000 customers, but traditional ATS)
- Orbio (new AI player, $7.6M funded, enterprise-focused)
- talentsconnect (WhatsApp-native competitor from Germany)

**Quick win sectors:**
1. Staffing agencies (Adecco, Randstad, Gi Group)
2. Tourism/hospitality chains
3. Retail (Mercadona, Inditex)

**Entry strategy:** Target staffing agencies as beachhead, build seasonal hiring expertise

---

## 🇵🇹 Portugal - ★★★☆☆ (MEDIUM OPPORTUNITY)

**Why it's viable:**
- 84% skills shortage (highest in Western Europe)
- 90% WhatsApp penetration
- 200,000 foreign workers needed (multilingual recruiting demand)

**Key competitors:**
- Paradox (enterprise, no local presence)
- Talkpush (WhatsApp-native, direct competitor)
- No dominant local player = opportunity

**Quick win sectors:**
1. BPO/outsourcing (high volume, sophisticated buyers)
2. Tourism (seasonal hiring)
3. Tech scale-ups (fast growth, limited HR)

**Entry strategy:** Enter via BPO sector first (clear ROI, volume hiring)

---

## 🔥 Key Competitive Insights

**Direct WhatsApp Threats:**
- **Talkpush** - Nearly identical to Klaaryo, proven scale (24,000 hires in 75 days)
- **talentsconnect** - Germany-based WhatsApp-native, 82% completion rate

**Enterprise Players (less direct competition):**
- Paradox, Gupy, Factorial - leave mid-market gap
- Higher pricing, longer sales cycles
- Klaaryo can own SME segment

**Your Advantages:**
1. WhatsApp-first design (not bolt-on integration)
2. Mid-market sweet spot (100-1,000 employees)
3. High-volume specialization (retail, hospitality, logistics)
4. EU compliance built-in (GDPR, AI Act)
5. Proven Italian success (La Piadineria case study)
