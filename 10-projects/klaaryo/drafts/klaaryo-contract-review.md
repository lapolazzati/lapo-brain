**1. Commission Scope (Section 5.2)**
> "10% commission on the Year-1 contract value (ACV) of every deal closed with a client he introduced or materially accelerated — no cap, no tiers, no distinction between first deal and subsequent ones."
> "It applies to all contracts introduced by Lapo, **in Brazil or any other market**."

✅ **This is good**: Uncapped, applies globally (not just Brazil), and "materially accelerated" is broad enough to cover your contributions.

❌ **Watch out**: "Year-1 contract value only" — renewals/expansions not included. If you help close a €25K deal that becomes €100K in Year 2, you only get 10% of the first €25K.

**Recommendation**: This is standard for pilot agreements. If you renew for 6+ months, negotiate ongoing revenue share or expansion commission.
