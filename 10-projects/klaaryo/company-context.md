## Product Offering

### Core Platform
Klaaryo is a conversational AI platform that automates HR processes through AI-powered agents operating on WhatsApp Business and other existing communication channels.

**Architecture**: Operates as both SaaS and API, serving as an orchestration layer that integrates above existing ATS (Applicant Tracking System) and HCM (Human Capital Management) systems.

### Primary Solutions

#### 1. Recruiting
- Manages entire hiring pipeline from initial contact through final feedback
- Conversational pre-screening for rapid candidate evaluation
- Automatic interview scheduling and reminders
- Candidate reactivation campaigns from existing databases
- Handles candidate questions about contract type, working hours, salary, and benefits

#### 2. Onboarding
- Guides new hires through paperwork and processes via conversational flow
- Digital document collection and e-signature in chat
- Automated onboarding workflow management

#### 3. Employee Engagement
- Maintains ongoing conversations with employees
- Sentiment surveys and pulse checks
- HR request handling
- Performance feedback collection
- Employee dissatisfaction detection to reduce turnover

### Key Features
- **WhatsApp-Native**: For each new job offer, AI agent starts conversations with candidates and analyzes their skills directly via WhatsApp
- **24/7 Automation**: AI collaborates with teams around the clock
- **Automated Interview Management**: Organizes interviews automatically, handles rescheduling and cancellations
- **Customization**: AI agent aligns with company's communication style and candidate evaluation criteria
- **Zero Friction Setup**: Automatic data synchronization across HR systems
- **Native Integration**: WhatsApp Business and major ATS/HCM systems

## Technology & Differentiators

### Technology Stack
- **AI Foundation**: Large Language Models (LLMs) for conversational AI
- **Primary Channel**: WhatsApp Business API (WABA) for automated and scalable communications
- **Integration**: Native connections with existing ATS and HCM systems
- **Deployment**: Available as SaaS platform and API
- **Target**: Optimized for high-volume frontline hiring and distributed workforce management

### Key Differentiators
- **WhatsApp-first approach**: Meets candidates and employees where they already communicate
- **Conversational AI specialization**: Designed specifically for HR processes rather than generic chatbots
- **Enterprise-grade integration**: Works above existing HR tech stack as orchestration layer
- **Frontline focus**: Specialized for field workers, retail, hospitality, and other distributed workforces
- **Agentic AI**: Autonomous agents that can handle complex multi-step processes

### Security & Compliance
- **ISO 27001 certified**
- **GDPR compliant**
- **EU AI Act ready**
- **Data Privacy**: No real data used for AI training
- **Client Control**: Client data remains proprietary and controlled exclusively by users
