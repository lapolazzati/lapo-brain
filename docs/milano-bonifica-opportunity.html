Process Overview: Bonifica Procedure

  1. Preliminary Environmental Investigation: Site assessment to determine contamination levels. Must be submitted via "Company in a Day" portal as of March 30, 2026.
  2. Site Characterization: Detailed analysis if contamination exceeds thresholds (CSC - Concentrazioni Soglia di Contaminazione).
  3. Risk Analysis: If CSC exceeded, determine risk-based cleanup targets (CSR - Concentrazioni Soglia di Rischio).
  4. Remediation Plan: Develop and submit bonifica plan to Comune and ARPA.
  5. Approval: Comune/Conference of Services approves plan.
  6. Execution: Implement bonifica interventions.
  7. Validation: ARPA validates results and certifies successful remediation.
  8. Site Release: Comune issues certification of completed remediation.

Timeline: Typical bonifica procedure from investigation to certification: 18-36 months depending on site complexity.
